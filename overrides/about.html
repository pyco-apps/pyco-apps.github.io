PYCO is the company facade for Liam Collod. A French artist making pictures for 9 years now. He decided a few years ago to learn the dark art of building software in the hope that it could improve his craft, but has instead found himself fully absorbed in it with the consequence of not really crafting pictures anymore.
He hopes to justify this hazardous life path by sharing his software with others so that maybe, those could actually improve their craft and still find pleasure in the ever-evolving realm that is digital art-making.
